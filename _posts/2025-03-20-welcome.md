Welcome! I finally got around to setting up a proper GitHub page for myslef. 

## What this site is

This is my personal webiste. I plan to use it for two main things:

1. **Projects** — a showcase of things I've built, with notes on how and why I built them.
2. **Blog** — technical writing, tutorials, and occasional thoughts on software development.

## What to expect

Posts will cover topics like:

- Robotics and ML problems
- Optimization methods
- Mathematical topics I'm interested in (geometry, algebraic geometry, etc.)
- Just interesting problems I've encountered

Some posts will be long-form deep-dives; others will be short notes. Quality over quantity.

## AI Policy
I'll use AI for research and help with the projects. At the same time the posts themselves are completely **AI-FREE** and written by me, since I want them to reflect my own experiences and thoughts.
