Welcome! I finally got around to setting up a proper home on the internet.

## What this site is

This is my personal corner of the web. I plan to use it for two main things:

1. **Projects** — a showcase of things I've built, with notes on how and why I built them.
2. **Blog** — technical writing, tutorials, and occasional thoughts on software development.

## What to expect

Posts will cover topics like:

- Systems programming and low-level concepts
- Web development patterns and practices
- Tools and productivity tips
- Interesting problems I've encountered

Some posts will be long-form deep-dives; others will be short notes. Quality over quantity.

## The stack

The site is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on GitHub Pages — free, fast, and simple. No databases, no CMS, just Markdown files and a bit of HTML/CSS.

Feel free to explore the [projects](/projects) section or subscribe to the [RSS feed](/feed.xml).
